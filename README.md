# AI Role Interview Guide

A comprehensive guide for AI/ML interviews covering multiple roles and specializations.

## Repository Structure

### role-overview/
Contains detailed overviews and role descriptions for different positions:
- `ml-engineer.md` - Machine Learning Engineer role
- `ai-engineer.md` - AI Engineer role
- `ai-research-engineer.md` - AI Research Engineer role
- `ai-architect.md` - AI Architect role
- `data-scientist.md` - Data Scientist role

### interview-questions/
Role-specific interview questions organized by position:
- `ml-engineer/` - ML Engineer questions
- `ai-engineer/` - AI Engineer questions
- `ai-research-engineer/` - AI Research Engineer questions
- `ai-architect/` - AI Architect questions
- `data-scientist/` - Data Scientist questions

### system-design/
System design resources:
- `ml-systems.md` - ML systems design
- `ai-product-systems.md` - AI product systems design
- `research-pipelines.md` - Research pipeline design

### evaluation-and-metrics/
Evaluation criteria and metrics for assessment

### deployment-and-ops/
Deployment strategies and operational considerations

### ethics-and-risk/
Ethics and risk assessment for AI systems

### resources/
Additional resources and references

## Getting Started

Browse the role-overview directory to understand the different AI/ML roles, then explore role-specific interview questions.
